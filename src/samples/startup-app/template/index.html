My Million Dollar Startup Idea

An AI-powered learning coach that helps teenagers discover their talents, build real skills, and land their first opportunity — all through personalised micro-challenges.

🔍 The Problem

Millions of teenagers have no idea what they're good at or where to start. School doesn't always show you the way.

  • 75% of teens feel unprepared for the real world after school
  • Most career guidance is generic, outdated, or just plain boring
  • Existing platforms talk at teens, not with them
  • No personalised roadmap from "I don't know" to "I got this"

💡 Our Solution

SkillSpark AI is a conversational AI coach that meets teens where they are and guides them step-by-step through skill discovery and real-world projects.

How it works

  1. Teen completes a fun 2-minute AI-powered interest quiz
  2. AI generates a personalised skill map and learning path
  3. Daily 10-minute micro-challenges keep momentum going
  4. Earn badges and build a shareable portfolio of real work
  5. Get matched with internships, competitions, and mentors
"Think Duolingo meets LinkedIn — but designed from day one for Gen Z."

📈 Market Opportunity

  • 1.2 billion teenagers worldwide
  • Ed-tech market worth $400B by 2028
  • Gen Z spends 7+ hours/day on screens
  • Employers desperate for skilled young talent
  • Parents willing to pay for career-ready kids

🏆 Why We Win

  • AI personalisation at the core — not bolted on
  • Built by teens, for teens (we get the language)
  • Gamified and mobile-first experience
  • Free tier to grow + premium for serious learners
  • B2B2C: schools & parents pay too

✨ Key Features

Skill Discovery Engine

An adaptive quiz that uses AI to map interests to in-demand skills like coding, design, content creation, business, and more.

Daily Micro-Challenges

Bite-sized tasks you can finish in 10 minutes that build real, portfolio-worthy skills over time.

AI Study Buddy

A friendly AI chat assistant that answers questions, explains concepts, and keeps you on track — available 24/7.

Portfolio Builder

Every completed challenge adds to a shareable profile that you can show to schools, employers, or competitions.

💰 Business Model

  • Free tier: Core skill discovery + 3 daily challenges
  • Premium (£7.99/month): Unlimited challenges, mentorship, AI tutor
  • School licences: £2/student/year for classroom integration
  • Employer partnerships: Pay to post opportunities to our talent pool

Target: 100,000 free users in Year 1, 10% conversion = £960K ARR
